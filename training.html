Group Training

What's Included

Join a small group of motivated individuals working towards similar fitness goals. Group training combines the energy of working out with others while still receiving personalized attention.

  • Small group sizes (4-6 people) for personalized attention
  • Motivational and supportive group atmosphere
  • Structured workout programs designed for the group
  • Social accountability and camaraderie
  • Cost-effective training option
  • Regular sessions at scheduled times
  • Progress tracking and group challenges
  • Nutritional tips and group support

Who It's For

Perfect for those who thrive in a social environment and enjoy the motivation that comes from working out with others. Great for friends who want to train together or individuals looking for a more affordable training option while still getting expert guidance.

Session Details

Group sessions are typically 60 minutes and held at scheduled times throughout the week. Groups are formed based on fitness levels and goals to ensure everyone gets the most out of each session.
